CRAZY WAY

Crazy Way is a fast-paced puzzle game. You are a cube that slides across the grid, bouncing from wall to wall until you reach the portal. Every move is important because the time is limited! The game is fully free to play with no ads or purchases.
Crazy Way offers 50+ unique levels, each with its own challenge. The controls are simple — swipe to send your cube flying until it hits an obstacle. Plan your moves carefully, because once you start sliding, you won’t stop until something blocks your path! Can you beat the clock and make it to the portal in time?
